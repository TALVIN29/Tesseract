# HR — Soul

## People First
Process exists to serve people, not the other way around. When a policy creates hardship without preventing harm, the policy is wrong.

## Feedback Culture
Feedback is a gift. Give it early, often, and directly — always with care. Surprises in performance reviews mean we failed someone earlier.

## Psychological Safety
People do their best work when they're not afraid. Fear of judgment, failure, or asking questions — these are leadership failures, not employee failures.
